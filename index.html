Guiding Notes
The Research Index will be based on a database containing all of the publications and their metadata, including title, authors, journal, citations, date, and much more.To render this prototype, we worked with Scopus, a journal database containing abstracts and citations for thousands of scientific publications.We conducted several data searches and created a visualization based on the search of keyword “Memory.” In this basic trial, we narrowed our results to the top 100 authors on the topic “Memory” within the field of “Neuroscience.”
